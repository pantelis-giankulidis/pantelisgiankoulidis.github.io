ABOUT ME

I graduated as an Electrical and Computer engineer from the Technical University of Crete in 2023. During my studies there, i created several projects focusing on computer and software engineering, the carrer path i am actually taking.
As an undergraduate student there, i was a member for more than a year of the traffic fluid project. The traffic fluid project focuses on autonomous vehicular mobility on environments where the stakeholders are fully or partially autonomous vehicles. Some of my contributions to the program include the proposal of an imporvement to the artificial potential fields, which is the area arround a vehicle where when another vehicle enters, it is more likely for a collision between them. The lane-free traffic environment requires more complex calculations for the artificial potential fields compare to the traditional traffic environment, because the vehicles can take any place in the road, they are not only inbound a lane.
